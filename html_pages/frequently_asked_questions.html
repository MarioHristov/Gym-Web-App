Frequently asked questions

General questions

  • 01

    How old do I need to be to visit the gym?

    To train in the fitness you need to be atleast 14 years old. If you are below 14 you must be accompanied by a parent or someone that is atleast 18 years old

  • 02

    What should I bring with me to the gym?

    You should always have a pair of clean shoes, which you will wear when you are there. Apart from that the every gym is equipped with lifting belts, resistance bands, lifting straps and etc.

  • 03

    Do I have to book a session with a coach beforehand?

    Yes. It is neccessary to book a session with a coach before you go to the gym, because the trainer might not be able to take more clients at your desired time.

  • 04

    Do I have to book a place for any event or activity?

    Yes. All activities organized by the gym itself have a person limit so you must book your place before you go to the activity. For events hosted by us or by users there may or may not be a limit, so you must check before you plan on participating. View more information about events here.

Membership questions

  • 05

    What is the price of a membership at Shredded Together?

    Shredded Together provides the user with various options they can choose from. The price will vary based on 3 factors: 1. The type of the membership (standard, premium, diamond)
    2. Certain age groups get discounts off of any of the above specified membership types
    3. The length of the membership (1 month, 3 months, 12 months) you get additional discounts if you choose between 3 months and 12 months variants.

  • 06

    Can I use MultiSport card at your gyms?

    Yes. If you have a MultiSport card you can visit any of our locations once a day and use any of the facilities in that gym.

  • 07

    Are there any other taxes applied except the gym membership?

    No. There aren't any other taxes except for the gym membership.

  • 08

    Are there any perks to purchasing a more expensive membership?

    Yes. You can view them onto the memberships page here.

  • 09

    What type of events are exclusive to members only?

    Some of the events we host are members only.
    For example: group trips, competitions (powerlifting, bodybuilding, gripMania, climbersParadice and many more), special public events. You can learn more about the events we host here.

  • 10

    Can I purchase or renew membership online?

    Yes. Memberships can be renewed, upgraded, purchased online through our website. You can view your membership details on your profile page here.
