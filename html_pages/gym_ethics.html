Our ethics

How to be a good gym bro/gal!

  1. Always use clean outfit when training.
  2. Say NO to dirty shoes. Always wear clean shoes!
  3. If you want to be an extra clean boy/gal, bring towel with you when training.
  4. Always put your equipment back into it's place. Your parents will be proud of you.
  5. Don't be a gym douchebag, always treat everyone with respect and don't yank the machines.
  6. Don't sleep on the equipment, there may be someone who wants to use it.
  7. Always ask if equipment you want to use is free.
  8. Don't hog multiple machines at the same time, be considerate.
  9. Keep the gym clean, treat it like your second home.
