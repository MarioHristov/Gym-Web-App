Have a MultiSport card?

Did you know that with it you can visit all Shredded Together clubs. You have the opportunity to enjoy your personal training without restrictions, as well as visit our group activities or spa areas.

With a Multisport card, you get unlimited access to the fitness areas, functional and cardio areas, one group class per day, as well as all group classes from the Les Mills programs. All Multisport card holders can visit our clubs every day from Monday to Sunday during their opening hours.

We are eager to see you!

Shredded Together staff
